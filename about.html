Company Overview

Who we are.

Collatz Labs was founded to answer a simple question: how does an organization prove a fact about its own cryptography to an auditor, customer, or regulator, without handing over the source code, keys, or business logic behind it?

We don't sell an algorithm — we don't compete with the open-source cryptography your engineers already trust. We orchestrate it. Collatz Labs is the platform enterprises use to discover what cryptography they're actually running, assess the quantum risk in it, plan and execute the migration, and keep managing it afterward — continuously, not as a one-time project.

That starts with our flagship product, zkAssess, and extends into the post-quantum migration and entropy foundations it feeds into.
