Why Encrypt

Our path to quantum safety, in four steps.

We don't start with a migration. We start with knowing what you're actually running — then match the fix to your systems, not the other way around.

Step 1 · Live

Analyze — zkAssess

Scan your existing code for quantum-vulnerable and classically weak cryptography before touching anything.

  • Deterministic, versioned pattern detection
  • Signed attestation — source never leaves your boundary
  • Score mapped to your compliance profile
Step 2 · Live

Choose Hybrid or Pure Post-Quantum Cryptography

Based on the zkAssess findings and your own requirements — timeline, risk tolerance, compliance regime — decide the right path.

  • Hybrid Post-Quantum Cryptography: classical + post-quantum, side by side
  • Pure Post-Quantum Cryptography: full replacement of vulnerable primitives
Step 3 · Live

Replace

Roll out the chosen algorithms across your systems, replacing the quantum-vulnerable primitives zkAssess flagged.

  • Staged rollout, system by system
  • No classical primitive left unaccounted for
Step 4 · Live

Test and finalize

Validate the replacement against real workloads, then sign off and finalize the migration.

  • Replacement testing against real workloads
  • Sign-off and finalized rollout
